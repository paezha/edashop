## Data Sets

### Auctions in a Distressed Real Estate Market in Italy

A data set with information about auctions of foreclosed properties in
Italy between 2000 and 2016.

- [`auctions_amf`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/auctions_amf.md)
  : Auctions in a Distressed Real Estate Market in Italy: Auction Market
  Features.
- [`auctions_pf`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/auctions_pf.md)
  : Auctions in a Distressed Real Estate Market in Italy: Profitability
  Features of Property.
- [`auctions_phy`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/auctions_phy.md)
  : Auctions in a Distressed Real Estate Market in Italy: Physical
  Features of Property.
- [`auctions_sef`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/auctions_sef.md)
  : Auctions in a Distressed Real Estate Market in Italy: Socio-Economic
  Features.

### Housing Price Gradients in Italian Capitals

A data set with information about housing price gradients in 112
provincial capitals in Italy.

- [`price_gradient`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/price_gradient.md)
  : Housing Price Gradients in Capital Cities in Italy.

### Industry Readiness Index 4.0

Data related to the Industry Readiness Index 4.0 for NUTS-2 regions in
Europe.

- [`i40_index_db`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/i40_index_db.md)
  : Database for Industry Readiness Index 4.0.
- [`i40_index_rank`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/i40_index_rank.md)
  : Promethee Rankings of Industry Readiness Index 4.0.

### Italian Vineyards

A sample of italian vineyards with landscape and management information.

- [`vineyards`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/vineyards.md)
  : A sample of Italian vineyards.

### Ph.D. Entrepreneurship in Italy

Data from a survey of Ph.D. students in Italy and their
entrepreneurship, including spinoffs, academic institutions, and regions
of Italy.

- [`phd_italy`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/phd_italy.md)
  : A qualitative survey on Ph.D. entrepreneurship in Italy.
- [`phd_italy_regions`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/phd_italy_regions.md)
  : Regional summary of survey on Ph.D. entrepreneurship in Italy.

### Geospatial Files

Files with geospatial information to complement the data sets.

- [`italy_nuts2`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/italy_nuts2.md)
  : NUTS-2 regions in Italy.
- [`nuts2`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/nuts2.md) :
  NUTS-2 regions.

### Sao Paulo census tracts

Data related to the Sao Paulo census tracts to be used in the Brazilian
edition.

- [`cntr_sp_ipvs`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/cntr_sp_ipvs.md)
  : Indice Paulista de Vulnerabilidade Social (IPVS) / São Paulo Social
  Vulnerability Index - Census tracts
- [`cntr_sp_head`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/cntr_sp_head.md)
  : Head of household - Census tracts
- [`cntr_sp_basico`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/cntr_sp_basico.md)
  : Basico - Census tracts
- [`cntr_sp_geo`](https://paezha.github.io/edashop/reference/cntr_sp_geo.md)
  : Census tract geometries
